Tutorial After Logging in, you should be redirected to the main menu. Click the Play button. You are presented with two options, search to play against another player, or play against a computer. Clicking on the "Search For Opponent" button will begin searching for another player. Clicking cancel will stop searching for opponents. Clicking on the "Play Computer" button will directly place you into the game against a CPU. Once an opponent has been found, or the "Play Computer" button has been pressed, you will be redirected to a battleship game! The left board will be your board, and the right board will be your opponent's baord. Click on a tile to place a ship down. The current ship being placed will be displayed by the notification bar at the bottom. The ships are color coded. Only valid ship placements are allowed. If you are unable to place a valid ship placement, then click on the "reset ships" button right below your board. Click on a tile to place a ship down. If you are unable to place a valid ship placement, then click on the "reset ships" button right below your board. Once ship placements are done, the game will notify the players that it has started and who's turn it currently is. Then, a green border will display on the whichever board's turn. Once a player win's, a popup will display showing who won.
